Prompt meaningful conversations

Check-ins ask quick, relevant questions to prompt conversations and highlight problems.

Managers - Check-ins are automatically scheduled and context is created for conversations.
Organisations - Checkin101 saves management time and ensures problems are tackled as they come up.

Save time, quickly

Gauges create urgency and help managers identify which staff need the most attention at any point in time.

Managers - Check-ins ensure every manager knows the status of the people they are responsible for.
Organisations - Checkin101 highlights trends to show when best to reengage staff with development initiatives and replicate the actions of successful managers.

Gauge who needs help

Check-ins can replace the time that is wasted in team meetings and create a safer environment to raise issues.

Managers - Check-ins create real-time data to identify what actions lead to better outcomes, and save time in evaluating their people.
Organisations - Checkin101 visualises what managers, teams and staff need the most attention to optimise management time.

Take faster action

Checkin101 helps initiate meaningful conversations with individual staff with the click of a button.

Managers - The real-time approach of Check-ins allow managers to identify and focus on coaching and development opportunities.
Organisation - Checkin101's frequent approach to feedback and conversations mean issues are resolved closer to the moment, instead of months after-the-fact.
