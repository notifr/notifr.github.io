Realtime Sentiment Analysis For Supporting Stakeholders

Checkin101 is the world's most flexible suite of pulse feedback tools for high-change environments.

We've taken our experience in achieving meaningful stakeholder buy-in and built a suite of tools that give your project managers a high level of data granularity, and your senior leaders a familiar high-level People KPI.

Top 3 features for high-change organisations:

Rapid Pulse Surveys

Let your project & implementation managers customise the questions they need, receive answers within the same day, and quickly target from 20 people to over 20,000.

Realtime Sentiment Analysis

Every employee & stakeholder interaction is securely stored, analysed in realtime and reported as a familiar sentiment metric, which forms a leading early indicator of pushback and resistance risks.

Flexible Segmentation

The entire tool suite, including employee segments, can be created, repurposed and modified as the organisation changes and new initiatives are required, whilst still generating consistant indicator reports and maintaining a single historical source for stakeholder sentiment.

Frequently Asked Questions

We often get asked a range of questions, from who uses the tool, to how the technology works:

Who uses the tool, how could it help my organisation?

We've worked with a number of managers who have found successful ways to use the tool suite - from projects involving stakeholders, distributed teams, training programmes and even breaking organisation silos.

Any stakeholder can respond to microsurveys straight from their email, and Project managers & sponsors have access to realtime dashboards.

Will Checkin101 work for my organisation?

Yes, we built the tool suite to be as flexible as possible. You can structure your stakeholder groups in the way that makes sense to you, and ask the questions that make the most sense to your culture.

We're all about delivering right information, to the right people, at the right time.

How long does it take to set up?

It takes less than 5 minutes to get an entire project set up - and what's more, we also work with you to get the best results possible and turn data into action.

Are you in the cloud?

Absolutely! The entire suite is easily accessible from anywhere in the world, meaning we're one of the best tools around for distributed environments where stakeholders are out in the field.

What about security and anonymity?

Our hosting facilities adhere to world-class security policies including proven, up-to-date firewall protection, intrusion detection systems, SSL encryption, and other security technologies.

We even give you control over your own data so you can decide who has visibility and which projects & departments need to remain anonymous.
